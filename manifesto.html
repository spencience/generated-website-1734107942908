The Manifesto of Destruction

In the age of infinite digital reproduction, true value lies in the act of destruction. Each piece destroyed is a statement against the perpetual accumulation of meaningless assets. We create through destruction, we value through elimination.

The Lamborghini wasn't just a car - it was a symbol of everything we've been conditioned to desire. Its destruction wasn't an end, but a genesis. A genesis of nothing.
